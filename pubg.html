INFORMATION

  • PUBG: Battlegrounds The game is based on previous mods that were created by Brendan "PlayerUnknown" Greene for other games, inspired by the 2000 Japanese film Battle Royale, and expanded into a standalone game under Greene's creative direction. In the game, up to one hundred players parachute onto an island and scavenge for weapons and equipment to kill others while avoiding getting killed themselves.
  • Platform(s): Microsoft Windows,Android,iOS,Xbox One,PlayStation 4,Stadia
  • Release: December 20, 2017
